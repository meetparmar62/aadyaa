Care Instructions

Washing Guidelines:

  • Machine wash in cold water (30°C or below) with similar colors
  • Use mild detergent to preserve the fabric's natural texture and colors
  • Avoid bleach, harsh chemicals, or fabric softeners
  • Turn the garment inside out before washing to protect the print

Drying & Storage:

  • Air dry in shade to prevent color fading
  • Avoid direct sunlight or high heat
  • Iron on medium heat while slightly damp for best results
  • Store in a cool, dry place with proper ventilation
  • Use padded hangers to maintain the garment's shape

Following these care instructions will help maintain the premium quality and vibrant colors of your kurti for years to come.
