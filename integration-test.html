üß™ Step-by-Step Testing Guide

Test Scenario 1: Add New Product

  1. Go to Add Product page
  2. Click "Generate ID" to create a new product ID
  3. Fill in all product details including cost price (ensure it's less than selling price)
  4. Add product images via Cloudinary widget
  5. Set stock quantity and minimum stock level
  6. Submit the form and verify the product appears in the admin list
  7. Check that profit margin is calculated correctly

Test Scenario 2: Customer Shopping Experience

  1. Go to Store Homepage
  2. Verify product cards show Product ID, stock status, and category
  3. Click on a product card to open the detailed modal
  4. In the modal, select different colors and sizes
  5. Adjust quantity and click "Add to Cart"
  6. Verify the notification appears and cart count updates
  7. Go to Cart page and verify the product is there with selected variants

Test Scenario 3: Purchase and Stock Management

  1. In the Cart, click "Demo Purchase" button
  2. Verify the success notification appears
  3. Go to Admin Dashboard
  4. Check that inventory statistics are updated
  5. Verify the purchased product shows reduced stock
  6. Check if any low stock alerts appear
  7. Use the stock update modal to adjust inventory

Test Scenario 4: Analytics and Reporting

  1. Browse products on store page (this should increment view analytics)
  2. Add products to cart (this should increment cart_add analytics)
  3. Complete demo purchases (this should increment purchase analytics)
  4. Go to admin dashboard and check analytics section
  5. Verify category breakdown and inventory values are correct
